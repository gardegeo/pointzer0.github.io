The Space Specifications

Accessible

Point Zero will be located in the centre of Thessaloniki

Spacious

We will aim to rent a place at least 100m2

Versatile

Three main areas, one of which should be able to re-purpose for meetup gatherings of at least 70 seated people

Convenient

A kitchen, a beverage bar, a clean and cozy bathroom.
We'll aim for a meeting room too!
